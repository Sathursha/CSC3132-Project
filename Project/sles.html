Sri Lanka Engineering Service (SLES)

Welcome to the Sri Lanka Engineering Service (SLES) page! Here you will find details about the roles, responsibilities, and career paths for engineers in the public sector.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Civil Engineers: Infrastructure planning and development
  • Mechanical Engineers: Machinery design and maintenance
  • Electrical Engineers: Power system management
  • Chief Engineers: Oversee engineering projects and teams

Eligibility Criteria

To join the Sri Lanka Engineering Service, candidates must meet the following criteria:

  • A degree in engineering from a recognized university
  • Pass the competitive SLES examination
  • Fulfill specific age and residency requirements
